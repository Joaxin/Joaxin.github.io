Intouchables

I remembered another movie, the most impressive and beautiful friendships on my list of watched movies, Philippe and Driss.
The original soundtrack, Una Mattina, postlude of intouchables, composed by Ludovico Einaudi, was touching and gorgeous.
we seem to be very far, actually, the soul is very close.

Je me suis rappelé un autre film, les amitiés les plus impressionnantes et les plus belles de ma liste de films regardés, Philippe et Driss.
La bande originale du film, Una Mattina, postlude of intouchables, composée par Ludovico Einaudi, était touchante et magnifique.
Nous semblons être très loin, en réalité, l’âme est très proche.

Hilda

I absolutley love everything about Hilda S01 by Netflix !
With full of fantasy creatures, Twig - Hilda’s loyal cute pet deer-fox, Wood man - Hilda’s mysterious smart friend, Alfur - Hilda’s best elf-friend, and the faithful giant, the thundering black crow, the terrific troll rock, the wandering lnightmare spirit , the homeless Nisse, the huge black hound, &c.
The characters and arts are creative,imaginative and fun, can’t wait to hear form season 2 or ep 14 soon.
