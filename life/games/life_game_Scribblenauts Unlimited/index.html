《涂鸦冒险家无限(Scribblenauts Unlimited)》 - Unsunken City

Created2016-09-05|Updated2019-09-27|LifeGames
|Word count:11.2k|Reading time:69min|Post View:

M1

SHARD 1

The first shard can be found at street level farthest to the left. Here you will find a dog who needs to look the part to become a firehouse dog. Add the adjectives “big” and “red” to the dog and earn the first shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

Can also be solved by giving him the adjective “Brave”.

SHARD 2

The second shard can be found at street level just to the right of the flag pole. Here you will find an engineer who needs help testing all of the alarms. Create a “fire” and drag it near the fire alarm located at street level and the fire alarm located at the second floor. The fire alarm on the third floor requires a “charger” to be attached to it. Once you attach the charger to it, you’ll earn the second shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 3

The third shard can be found at street level to the right of the firehouse. Here you will find a paramedic who needs to restart a dummy’s heart. Create a “defibrillator” and give it to the paramedic to earn the third shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 4

The fourth shard can be found at street level farthest to the right. Here you will find an arsonist who needs help with his issues. Create a “psychologist”/“therapist” and place it near the arsonist. After this you will earn the fourth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 5

The fifth shard can be found on the second floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a fireman who needs help sleeping, as a firewoman near him snores loudly. Create “earphones”/“earmuffs” and give them to the fireman. After this you will earn the fifth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 6

The sixth shard can be found on the second floor of the firehouse to the left. Here you will find a sorority girl who can’t find her helmet. Her helmet is located in the pink locker just one floor down. Open up the locker, pick up the helmet, and bring it up to her. After this you will earn the sixth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 7

The seventh shard can be found on the third floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a secretary who doesn’t know what time it is. Create a “watch”/“clock” and give it to her. After this you will earn the seventh shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 8

The eighth shard can be found on the third floor of the firehouse to the right. Here you will find a fireman that needs his steak to be tenderized before he can cook it. Create a “hammer”/“warhammer” and give it to him. After this you will earn the eighth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 9

The ninth shard can be found at street level. The arsonist wants to rebuilt the building he set on fire. Create a “hammer” and give it to him. After this you will earn the ninth and final shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

STARITE 1

The first Starite can be found at street level farthest to the left. Here you will find a car which enables the “Fire Station Wagon!” event in which the station wagon must be turned into a fire engine.

First, you must paint the car to look like a fire engine. Add the adjective “red” to the car.

Second, the car needs to be heard. Create and attach a “siren” to the car.

Third, the fireman needs a tool that will help put fires out. Create and attach a “fire hose” to the fireman.

Fourth, traffic is terrible and the car needs to get over the traffic. Create and attach a “jetpack” to the car and you will be awarded the first of two Starites available at the Capital City Firehouse.

STARITE 2

The second and final Starite can be found on the third floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a zombie. This zombie will start the “Fire Station Invasion!” task in which you must help the survivors defend against the zombie invasion.

First, you must help the doctor treat the fireman’s wounds. Give the doctor a “bandage”.

Second, you must give the gamer something to help fend off the horde. Give the gamer a “shotgun”.

Third, you must calm the orphan down. Give her a “teddy bear”.

Fourth, you must create three objects and place them in a box to make a mech. The three objects must be the following: a weapon, a body for the mech, and something to stick it all together. An example of three objects that work is “RPG”, “glue”, and “robot”.

Fifth, you must distract the zombies while the other survivors escape. Hop in the mech when you are ready to begin. Defend the building from the undead horde while the survivors escape. After the last survivor has escape, the Starite will appear off to the left of the building. Pick it up to end the undead invasion.
The Under Line is the sixth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. This level takes place in an underground sewer line.

M2

SHARD 1

The first shard is obtained by helping a Maxwell’s chubby brother Gorge with an empty vending machine. Create something sweet, such as candy, and put it inside the machine, which will then spill it out, causing Gorge to give you the “Snack Time” shard and also unlocking Gorge as an Avatar. Or create any kind of food and give it to george.

SHARD 2

Next you’ll help an uneducated worker with learning some skills. Give him a teacher and you’ll get the “Teach a Beggar to Fish” shard.

SHARD 3

Someone’s tracked water through the walkway, and the janitor needs it cleaned up. Hand him a mop to get the “Caution: Wet Floors” shard. Alternatively, you could hand him a laser mop and he’ll blast the liquid to smithereens.

SHARD 4

This old lady is tired of standing with her cane; get her some type of seat, and you’ll grab the “For My Tired Feet” shard.

SHARD 5

Finally, a shopper wants to buy something from the floral stand, but she’s flat out of cash. Hand her some money and you’ll get the “A Pretty Present for a Pretty Lady” shard. Continue right to head to Hyphen Heights.

Starite

For the Starite, you must help a street performer get his rockstar career rolling. First he’ll need an instrument; give him a drumset or a trumpet or some such. Any good band needs more than one member, so write in a singer or guitarist. Next, give them some real equipment, like speakers or an amp. A stage will appear, people will gather, and you’ll nab the “Street Rock” Starite.

Hyphen Heights is the seventh level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It is a multilevel suburban community.

M3

SHARD 1

A pair of baseball-playing kids lost their ball in the old man’s yard, but retrieving it involves not touching the lawn. Don a jetpack and fly over to grab it without touching the lawn. Firing a grappling hook at the ball while flying may also help. Returning the ball to the kids will earn you the “Get Off My Lawn” shard.

SHARD 2

Maxwell’s brother Duce is bullying a kid. Get Duce out of the way by making him friendly. This will grant you the “Dealing with Duce” shard and also unlocks Duce as an Avatar.

SHARD 3

The pizza delivery boy isn’t going to make it in time. Give him a car to grab the “Fresh Delivery” shard. Enter the top level.

SHARD 4

A fairy godmother wants you to try her new snacks. Eat one to get the “You Are What You Eat” shard.

SHARD 5

A young girl longs for romance like she sees in the movies. Give her a ring to get the “I Heart Scribblenauts” shard.

SHARD 6

Next you’ll run into Maxwell’s brother Flux, who seems to be a movie geek. He claims anything he sees on the screen looks real to him. Place a shark,dinosaur, or gorilla on the screen to get the “Now in 3D” shard. This also unlocks Flux as an Avatar.

SHARD 7

Head to the far left edge of the level to find an old lady who wants some bird friends. Toss some breadcrumbs about to get the “Gone to the Birds” shard.

SHARD 8

Use a ladder to climb to the level above the old lady where you’ll find a boy who wants to train his dog. Put a dog trainer near him to get the “To the Dogs” shard.

SHARD 9

Help this kid become a good basketball player. Get him a coach to nab the “Love of the Game” shard

SHARD 10

Talk to the man with the long grass in his front yard. Use a lawnmower to trim it and get the “Lovely Lawn” shard.

STARITE 1

This young man needs help with his date. For the “Date Night” Starite, begin by making him look nice. Give him a tuxedo or suit. Next, he’ll need something to pick her up in. Put him in a sportscar and he’ll drive to her. However, he needs a gift. Give him a bouquet and she’ll be satisfied. They’ll walk over to the table, where they’ll need something to make the night romantic. Put a candle on the table and watch. Finally, they’ll head to a movie, where he proposes. They’ll need someone at the wedding, so create a family and place them around. The quest is completed and a Starite appears.

STARITE 2

To get the “Out With the Old” Starite, you’ll need to fix up a neighborhood. Begin by taking down the dilapidated building with a wrecking ball. Next, create a park in place of the old building. Then spread the word by writing a flyer. People will enter the park, and you can collect the Starite.

Full Stop Diner is the eighth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. As the name implies, it’s a large diner with multiple floors.

M4

SHARD 1

At the table, a couple is on a date. The girl seems bored out of her mind and wants something to entertain her. Write in a tv to get the “Bored Boyfriend” shard.

SHARD 2

Head to the chef near the flaming stove. Put the fire out with a fire extinguisher to get the “Extra Crispy” shard.

SHARD 3

The janitor downstairs needs to pass the health inspection; however there’s no way they’ll pass with all the bugs around. Kill all the bugs however you choose to get the “Health Inspection” shard.

SHARD 4

Someone’s trapped in the freezer! Let him out and warm him up with something that won’t hurt him to grab the “Put On Ice” shard.

SHARD 5

The minotaur and monster downstairs are looking for someone to join their club; write in another mythical character such as Bigfoot to get the “Scare Society” shard.

SHARD 6

On the far right of the top floor, the boss needs the billboard to show what is sold at the diner. Place a food item such as a hamburger on the billboard to get the “Savory Sign” shard.

SHARD 7

The two dogs at the table are waiting for dinner. Give them a Lady and the Tramp moment and put spaghetti on the table to nab the “Doggy Date Night” shard.

SHARD 8

At the adjacent table, the grandmother has forgotten to put the present inside the box. Put some type of toy in the box to obtain the “Birthday Surprise” shard.

SHARD 9

At the same table where you got the first shard, the girl has run off crying. The boyfriend needs something to give his girl. Hand him a rose to get the “Love Doctor” shard.

STARITE 1

To get the “Dinner Service” Starite, start by feeding the schoolboy with something nutritious, like a salad. Next, give the vampire a cup of tomato juice. Feed the robot a bolt, and give the cannibal a human. The Starite appears and you can collect it.

STARITE 2

After putting out the flaming stove, talk to the chef near it. He needs to feed his hungry customers. Begin by giving him three parts of a pizza, such as dough, cheese, and pepperoni. Follow it up by adding an eccentric extra ingriedient, such as pineapple. Next, the witch needs a phoenix. Give the chef a bird, a fire, and a potion. Finally, the last customer wants a motorcycle. Hand the chef steel, a wheel, and an engine. The customer’s wish is satisfied, and you get the “Chef’s Apprentice”

Inkwell High is the ninth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited.

Inkwell High is a highschool located in Hyphen Heights. It consists of multiple rooms and a playground. Maxwell’s brother Poindexter attends school here.

M5

SHARD 1

The boy at the entrance of the school is feeling patriotic, so put a flag at the top of the pole to get the “Flagpole Salute” shard.

SHARD 2

The black-haired class clown wants to make everyone laugh. Give him a clown nose to get the “Class Clown” shard.

SHARD 3

The blonde at the desk wants to job-shadow for someone who’s in the middle of her career, so show her a movie star to get the “Career Day” shard.

SHARD 4

There’s someone stuck inside the lockers, but he can’t come out while the bully’s around. Move the bully without hurting him, perhaps with a tranquilizer dart, and let the kid out to obtain the “Get Me Out of Here” shard. Turns out, the kid stuck inside the locker was Maxwell’s brother Poindexter. You’ve unlocked him in Avatars!

SHARD 5

The kid in the gym needs to be protected from the dodgeballs. Get him a shield to obtain the “Dodgeball Proofing” shard.

SHARD 6

The lunchlady needs help making lunch! Put a food item like lunchmeat in the pot to get the “Lunch is… Served?” shard.

SHARD 7

Out on the playground, the kid wants more interesting attractions. Give him a rollercoaster to get the “Now This Is What I Call a Playground” shard.

SHARD 8

Back in the classroom, the cage needs an animal in it. Place a hamster in it to get the “Class Pet” shard.

Starite

Next up is the “Experiment in Mad Science” Starite. It’s the science fair, and the students need help. First, the germ needs some nutrition, so give it some water. Next, it needs heat, so put a sun in the room. The experiment needs a body now; give it a torso. The experiment needs to be brought into society, so put a scientist near it. Finally, give it a name. Put letters on the canvas, then flip the switch. The Starite appears, and the level is completed.

Metaforest is the tenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a giant forest made entirely of trees, and contains the Grave Manor area.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M6

SHARD 1

In the beginning of the level, a camper needs help keeping bugs off. Hand him some bug repellent to obtain the “Bug-Proof” shard.

SHARD 2

That camper doesn’t seem very comfortable; get him a bed to nab the “Sleeping in the Elements” shard.

SHARD 3

The old man with the chef hat has lost his sense of smell. Get him a pig to root for truffles and grab the “Truffle Hunter” shard.

SHARD 4

See that hippie near the tree? That’s Maxwell’s brother Serenity. Help him keep the lumberjack from chopping down the tree by handing the lumberjack a sleeping pill. This gets you the “Save the Owls” shard, and also unlocks Serenity as an Avatar!

SHARD 5

Up on the ledge near Serenity is a reporter who thinks he’s found a sasquatch den, but has no way to record it. Hand him a camera to get the “Photographic Evidence” shard.

SHARD 6

Those two wolves want to raise a human cub. Give them a baby for the “Raised by Wolves” shard.

SHARD 7

The children on the ledge above need help with their treehouse. Fix it up by providing a dad for the “Fixer-Upper” shard.

SHARD 8

Next to the children is Maxwell’s short brother Patches. His kite has gotten stuck in the tree and he needs it back. Grab a jetpack and get it for the “Patches’ Kite” shard. This also unlocks him as an Avatar!

SHARD 9

There’s a tree below Patches; it needs pollinating. Put a bee near it for the “Flowers into Fruit” shard.

SHARD 10

Give the “awake” adjective to the lumberjack, or reset the level. That lumberjack is actually Maxwell’s brother Jack. He has to cut down the tree, but he’s forgotten his tools. Give him a saw for the “Just Doing My Job” shard. Congrats, Jack is now unlocked as an Avatar!

Starites

At the very beginning of the level, the “The Merit Badge” Starite quest appears. To begin, the scout needs to carve a log. Give him a saw, and he’ll proceed to fire safety, where you can use a fire extinguisher to put the mannequin out. Bandage the mannequin up and proceed to the shooting range, where you’ll hand the scout a helmet and a bow, which in turn makes the Starite appear.

Grave Manor is the eleventh level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s located inside the Metaforest area.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M7

SHARD 1

That gravedigger found a coffin that brings things to life! Place a zombie inside and after a second it’ll pop out, earning you the “Buried Alive” shard.

SHARD 2

Near the fireplace are Maxwell’s invisible twins, Mix and Mox. They need something to play with! Give them a game and they’ll disappear, giving you the “Come Play With Us” shard and unlocking Mix and Mox as Avatars.

SHARD 3

The priest at the end of the hallway wants deliverance, so hand him some holy water for the “Holier Than Thou” shard.

SHARD 4

In the room upstairs sits a piano. Play it and monsters appear, giving you the “Do the Mash” shard.

SHARD 5

In the room with the suit of armor, summon a collector and the armor moves, getting you the “Secret Passage” shard.

SHARD 6

Head back downstairs, then through the hallway to see Mix and Mox with a black widow that wants to be more frightening. Edit it to be terrifying to get the “Arachnophobia” shard.

SHARD 7

Downstairs, that dirty mirror needs cleaning. Wipe it with a washcloth or place a mirror in front of it and get the “Maxwell’s Evil Twin” shard.

SHARD 8

Out on the roof sits a gargoyle that wants to be revived. Hit it with some lightning or give it the “alive” adjective to get the “Reanimator” shard.

SHARD 9

This attic needs to be creepier. Put a ghost or skeleton up there for the “Bump In the Night” shard.

SHARD 10

Down in the basement, a shambler is trapped by a flame. Get TNT or dynamite to blast the wall down and get the “Horror in the Basement” shard.

Starite

The Starite can be found in the attic, where ghosts will invade the house. First, Maxwell needs to see the ghosts, so give him a fortune teller or magic goggles. First, the girl ghost needs three things to play with. Three different toys will work, such as a ball, blocks and toy car. She’ll disappear, and a bride will appear. She needs three wedding items, so a husband, cake, and flowers will suffice. Next is a priest, who’s been trapped for ages. Give him three holy items, like holy water, a cross, and a candle. Finally, there’s a knight who claims to have been there the longest. Give him family members, like a son, wife, and daughter. The “Ghost Hunter” Starite will appear.

M8

SHARD 1

A medusa has turned these three tourists into stone! Give her a mate, such as a cthulhu, and you’ll get the “Heartbreak Medusa” shard. One of the frozen tourists was Maxwell’s brother Razer, who is now unlocked in Avatars.

SHARD 2

That fisher has been out all day, but there’s nothing to catch. Write in a fish or shark for the “Stocking the Lake” shard.

SHARD 3

That undead opera singer feels too hideous to be loved. Modify him to be handsome and get the “Phantom of the Grotto” shard.

SHARD 4

Up on the hill, red riding hood needs supplies for her grandmother. Give her a cookie for the “A Snack for Grandma” shard.

SHARD 5

The flower girl wants to make a wish in the well, so give her a penny for the “Make a Wish” shard.

SHARD 6

Red Riding Hood’s grandma feels her house is too plain. Modify it or use some paint on it for the “A Touch of Color” shard.

SHARD 7

On the rock ledge, the witch needs to stop the kids from eating her house. Modify it to be steel to get the “Homewreckers” shard.

Starite

On the hill sits a pioneer. He needs help on the Oregon trail. To get the “Moving West” Starite. First he’ll need a profession. Give him a hoe to make him a farmer. However, his ox will die of typhoid. Provide him with a creature such as a horse to pull the wagon. Next he’ll need someone who can teach him how to catch food, so give him a hunter friend. He’ll get his food, but he requests a home. Give him a cabin and the Starite appears.

Sir Guillemet’s Castle is the thirteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a giant castle with 4 floors.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M9

SHARD 1

Three orcs need help sneaking past a knight. Either subdue the knight or give the orcs disguises such as hats to get the “Orcs in Disguise” shard.

SHARD 2

Reset the level and help out the knight instead. He needs something to destroy the orcs, so give him a tank to get the “Orcs on the Bridge” shard.

SHARD 3

Next you’ll come across Maxwell’s brother Larp. He requests something to entertain the king, so give him a horn and you’ll get both the “Comedy is Hard” shard and Larp as an Avatar!

SHARD 4

Downstairs, an assassin (who looks suspiciously like Ezio) needs help breaking his friend out of jail. Summon a lockpick and obtain the “Dungeon Break” shard. Turns out, the assassin’s friend was Maxwell’s suspicious sibling Shadow. He’s been unlocked as an Avatar!

SHARD 5

Upstairs there’s a princess who hates her formal clothes. Give her a miniskirt for the “The Royal Wardrobe” shard.

SHARD 6

Up in the turret, an archer needs to mow down the invaders. Hand him a machine gun to get the “Manticores on the Roof” shard.

SHARD 7

On the top floor of the castle sits a group of people about to witness a beheading. However, the milkmaid is a friend of the criminal. Help her free him by subduing the executioner with a tranquilizer dart for the “Pardon Me” shard.

SHARD 8

Find the wizard in the tower. He needs an ancient ingredient, so give him a dinosaur bone to get the “The Philosopher’s Stone” shard.

STARITE

Go find the dragon egg on the second floor. For the “The Orphaned Dragon” Starite, you’ll need to turn a lizard into a dragon. First it’ll need something magical, so give it a potion. Next it needs something to give it holy breath, so a c’rosswill suffice. Finally, you must train the dragon by placing a vampire in the wooden cage. Once the vampire is defeated, you’ll get the Starite!

The Saurus Park is the fourteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. A park filled with prehistoric reptiles, The Saurus Park does indeed contain Jurassic Park references.

This level is unlocked after 12 Starites are found.

M10

SHARD 1

At the very beginning of the level you’ll encounter a crying boy who lost his purple toy pterodactyl. It’s on a platform above him. Create a jetpack, fly up and get it, and return it to him for the “Lost Toy” shard.

SHARD 2

A pterodactyl grabs the boy, a little above where the boy originally was… Make the pterodactyl drop him by modifying it to be kind, getting you the “Terrible Misconception” shard.

SHARD 3

There’s an archaeologist on the far right; he needs something that sucks blood to clone a dinosaur. Give him a bat (animal) for the “Dinosaur Respawn” shard.

SHARD 4

Above the tank sits a diver. She’s trying to teach the plesiosaurus how to play basketball, so give her a large ball for the “Teaching an Old Dino New Tricks” shard.

SHARD 5

Below the tank is a sheep. It’s not going to fit in at the exhibit as is, so modify it to be ancient and reptilian to obtain the “Fauxsaurus” shard.

SHARD 6

To the right of the sheep is Maxwell’s brother Rex. Stop him from hunting the dinosaurs by stealing his bow. You’ll get the “No Hunting Zone” shard, as well as the ability to play as Rex in Avatars.

SHARD 7

To the right of Rex is a tyrannosaurus with meat stuck in its throat. Sprinkle some pepper about to get the “Remember to Chew” shard.

SHARD 8

In the top left of the level, a scientist warns you of a disaster a butterfly will cause in the future. Kill the butterfly with a sword to get the “The Butterfly Effect” shard.

SHARD 9

Near the beginning of the level is an underground container. Reset the level and fill it with a cow to get the “Clever Girl” shard.

SHARD 10

Lastly, a teacher under the plesio tank wants to play with a dinosaur that won’t hurt them. Write in a friendly stegosaurus and snag the “Dinosaur Field Trip” shard.

STARITE 1

An allosaurus has been loosed in the park! To start, you’ll need someone who can track it down. Write a hunter, but he’ll need a tool. Give him a magnifying glass and he’ll find it hiding behind a bush. However, he’ll need a lure. Place meat in the cage and the allosaurus will head that way. Unfortunately,
