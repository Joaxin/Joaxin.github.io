《涂鸦冒险家无限(Scribblenauts Unlimited)》 - Unsunken City

Created2016-09-05|Updated2019-09-27|LifeGames
|Word count:11.2k|Reading time:69min|Post View:

M1

SHARD 1

The first shard can be found at street level farthest to the left. Here you will find a dog who needs to look the part to become a firehouse dog. Add the adjectives “big” and “red” to the dog and earn the first shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

Can also be solved by giving him the adjective “Brave”.

SHARD 2

The second shard can be found at street level just to the right of the flag pole. Here you will find an engineer who needs help testing all of the alarms. Create a “fire” and drag it near the fire alarm located at street level and the fire alarm located at the second floor. The fire alarm on the third floor requires a “charger” to be attached to it. Once you attach the charger to it, you’ll earn the second shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 3

The third shard can be found at street level to the right of the firehouse. Here you will find a paramedic who needs to restart a dummy’s heart. Create a “defibrillator” and give it to the paramedic to earn the third shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 4

The fourth shard can be found at street level farthest to the right. Here you will find an arsonist who needs help with his issues. Create a “psychologist”/“therapist” and place it near the arsonist. After this you will earn the fourth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 5

The fifth shard can be found on the second floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a fireman who needs help sleeping, as a firewoman near him snores loudly. Create “earphones”/“earmuffs” and give them to the fireman. After this you will earn the fifth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 6

The sixth shard can be found on the second floor of the firehouse to the left. Here you will find a sorority girl who can’t find her helmet. Her helmet is located in the pink locker just one floor down. Open up the locker, pick up the helmet, and bring it up to her. After this you will earn the sixth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 7

The seventh shard can be found on the third floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a secretary who doesn’t know what time it is. Create a “watch”/“clock” and give it to her. After this you will earn the seventh shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 8

The eighth shard can be found on the third floor of the firehouse to the right. Here you will find a fireman that needs his steak to be tenderized before he can cook it. Create a “hammer”/“warhammer” and give it to him. After this you will earn the eighth shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

SHARD 9

The ninth shard can be found at street level. The arsonist wants to rebuilt the building he set on fire. Create a “hammer” and give it to him. After this you will earn the ninth and final shard available at the Capital City Firehouse.

STARITE 1

The first Starite can be found at street level farthest to the left. Here you will find a car which enables the “Fire Station Wagon!” event in which the station wagon must be turned into a fire engine.

First, you must paint the car to look like a fire engine. Add the adjective “red” to the car.

Second, the car needs to be heard. Create and attach a “siren” to the car.

Third, the fireman needs a tool that will help put fires out. Create and attach a “fire hose” to the fireman.

Fourth, traffic is terrible and the car needs to get over the traffic. Create and attach a “jetpack” to the car and you will be awarded the first of two Starites available at the Capital City Firehouse.

STARITE 2

The second and final Starite can be found on the third floor of the firehouse. Here you will find a zombie. This zombie will start the “Fire Station Invasion!” task in which you must help the survivors defend against the zombie invasion.

First, you must help the doctor treat the fireman’s wounds. Give the doctor a “bandage”.

Second, you must give the gamer something to help fend off the horde. Give the gamer a “shotgun”.

Third, you must calm the orphan down. Give her a “teddy bear”.

Fourth, you must create three objects and place them in a box to make a mech. The three objects must be the following: a weapon, a body for the mech, and something to stick it all together. An example of three objects that work is “RPG”, “glue”, and “robot”.

Fifth, you must distract the zombies while the other survivors escape. Hop in the mech when you are ready to begin. Defend the building from the undead horde while the survivors escape. After the last survivor has escape, the Starite will appear off to the left of the building. Pick it up to end the undead invasion.
The Under Line is the sixth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. This level takes place in an underground sewer line.

M2

SHARD 1

The first shard is obtained by helping a Maxwell’s chubby brother Gorge with an empty vending machine. Create something sweet, such as candy, and put it inside the machine, which will then spill it out, causing Gorge to give you the “Snack Time” shard and also unlocking Gorge as an Avatar. Or create any kind of food and give it to george.

SHARD 2

Next you’ll help an uneducated worker with learning some skills. Give him a teacher and you’ll get the “Teach a Beggar to Fish” shard.

SHARD 3

Someone’s tracked water through the walkway, and the janitor needs it cleaned up. Hand him a mop to get the “Caution: Wet Floors” shard. Alternatively, you could hand him a laser mop and he’ll blast the liquid to smithereens.

SHARD 4

This old lady is tired of standing with her cane; get her some type of seat, and you’ll grab the “For My Tired Feet” shard.

SHARD 5

Finally, a shopper wants to buy something from the floral stand, but she’s flat out of cash. Hand her some money and you’ll get the “A Pretty Present for a Pretty Lady” shard. Continue right to head to Hyphen Heights.

Starite

For the Starite, you must help a street performer get his rockstar career rolling. First he’ll need an instrument; give him a drumset or a trumpet or some such. Any good band needs more than one member, so write in a singer or guitarist. Next, give them some real equipment, like speakers or an amp. A stage will appear, people will gather, and you’ll nab the “Street Rock” Starite.

Hyphen Heights is the seventh level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It is a multilevel suburban community.

M3

SHARD 1

A pair of baseball-playing kids lost their ball in the old man’s yard, but retrieving it involves not touching the lawn. Don a jetpack and fly over to grab it without touching the lawn. Firing a grappling hook at the ball while flying may also help. Returning the ball to the kids will earn you the “Get Off My Lawn” shard.

SHARD 2

Maxwell’s brother Duce is bullying a kid. Get Duce out of the way by making him friendly. This will grant you the “Dealing with Duce” shard and also unlocks Duce as an Avatar.

SHARD 3

The pizza delivery boy isn’t going to make it in time. Give him a car to grab the “Fresh Delivery” shard. Enter the top level.

SHARD 4

A fairy godmother wants you to try her new snacks. Eat one to get the “You Are What You Eat” shard.

SHARD 5

A young girl longs for romance like she sees in the movies. Give her a ring to get the “I Heart Scribblenauts” shard.

SHARD 6

Next you’ll run into Maxwell’s brother Flux, who seems to be a movie geek. He claims anything he sees on the screen looks real to him. Place a shark,dinosaur, or gorilla on the screen to get the “Now in 3D” shard. This also unlocks Flux as an Avatar.

SHARD 7

Head to the far left edge of the level to find an old lady who wants some bird friends. Toss some breadcrumbs about to get the “Gone to the Birds” shard.

SHARD 8

Use a ladder to climb to the level above the old lady where you’ll find a boy who wants to train his dog. Put a dog trainer near him to get the “To the Dogs” shard.

SHARD 9

Help this kid become a good basketball player. Get him a coach to nab the “Love of the Game” shard

SHARD 10

Talk to the man with the long grass in his front yard. Use a lawnmower to trim it and get the “Lovely Lawn” shard.

STARITE 1

This young man needs help with his date. For the “Date Night” Starite, begin by making him look nice. Give him a tuxedo or suit. Next, he’ll need something to pick her up in. Put him in a sportscar and he’ll drive to her. However, he needs a gift. Give him a bouquet and she’ll be satisfied. They’ll walk over to the table, where they’ll need something to make the night romantic. Put a candle on the table and watch. Finally, they’ll head to a movie, where he proposes. They’ll need someone at the wedding, so create a family and place them around. The quest is completed and a Starite appears.

STARITE 2

To get the “Out With the Old” Starite, you’ll need to fix up a neighborhood. Begin by taking down the dilapidated building with a wrecking ball. Next, create a park in place of the old building. Then spread the word by writing a flyer. People will enter the park, and you can collect the Starite.

Full Stop Diner is the eighth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. As the name implies, it’s a large diner with multiple floors.

M4

SHARD 1

At the table, a couple is on a date. The girl seems bored out of her mind and wants something to entertain her. Write in a tv to get the “Bored Boyfriend” shard.

SHARD 2

Head to the chef near the flaming stove. Put the fire out with a fire extinguisher to get the “Extra Crispy” shard.

SHARD 3

The janitor downstairs needs to pass the health inspection; however there’s no way they’ll pass with all the bugs around. Kill all the bugs however you choose to get the “Health Inspection” shard.

SHARD 4

Someone’s trapped in the freezer! Let him out and warm him up with something that won’t hurt him to grab the “Put On Ice” shard.

SHARD 5

The minotaur and monster downstairs are looking for someone to join their club; write in another mythical character such as Bigfoot to get the “Scare Society” shard.

SHARD 6

On the far right of the top floor, the boss needs the billboard to show what is sold at the diner. Place a food item such as a hamburger on the billboard to get the “Savory Sign” shard.

SHARD 7

The two dogs at the table are waiting for dinner. Give them a Lady and the Tramp moment and put spaghetti on the table to nab the “Doggy Date Night” shard.

SHARD 8

At the adjacent table, the grandmother has forgotten to put the present inside the box. Put some type of toy in the box to obtain the “Birthday Surprise” shard.

SHARD 9

At the same table where you got the first shard, the girl has run off crying. The boyfriend needs something to give his girl. Hand him a rose to get the “Love Doctor” shard.

STARITE 1

To get the “Dinner Service” Starite, start by feeding the schoolboy with something nutritious, like a salad. Next, give the vampire a cup of tomato juice. Feed the robot a bolt, and give the cannibal a human. The Starite appears and you can collect it.

STARITE 2

After putting out the flaming stove, talk to the chef near it. He needs to feed his hungry customers. Begin by giving him three parts of a pizza, such as dough, cheese, and pepperoni. Follow it up by adding an eccentric extra ingriedient, such as pineapple. Next, the witch needs a phoenix. Give the chef a bird, a fire, and a potion. Finally, the last customer wants a motorcycle. Hand the chef steel, a wheel, and an engine. The customer’s wish is satisfied, and you get the “Chef’s Apprentice”

Inkwell High is the ninth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited.

Inkwell High is a highschool located in Hyphen Heights. It consists of multiple rooms and a playground. Maxwell’s brother Poindexter attends school here.

M5

SHARD 1

The boy at the entrance of the school is feeling patriotic, so put a flag at the top of the pole to get the “Flagpole Salute” shard.

SHARD 2

The black-haired class clown wants to make everyone laugh. Give him a clown nose to get the “Class Clown” shard.

SHARD 3

The blonde at the desk wants to job-shadow for someone who’s in the middle of her career, so show her a movie star to get the “Career Day” shard.

SHARD 4

There’s someone stuck inside the lockers, but he can’t come out while the bully’s around. Move the bully without hurting him, perhaps with a tranquilizer dart, and let the kid out to obtain the “Get Me Out of Here” shard. Turns out, the kid stuck inside the locker was Maxwell’s brother Poindexter. You’ve unlocked him in Avatars!

SHARD 5

The kid in the gym needs to be protected from the dodgeballs. Get him a shield to obtain the “Dodgeball Proofing” shard.

SHARD 6

The lunchlady needs help making lunch! Put a food item like lunchmeat in the pot to get the “Lunch is… Served?” shard.

SHARD 7

Out on the playground, the kid wants more interesting attractions. Give him a rollercoaster to get the “Now This Is What I Call a Playground” shard.

SHARD 8

Back in the classroom, the cage needs an animal in it. Place a hamster in it to get the “Class Pet” shard.

Starite

Next up is the “Experiment in Mad Science” Starite. It’s the science fair, and the students need help. First, the germ needs some nutrition, so give it some water. Next, it needs heat, so put a sun in the room. The experiment needs a body now; give it a torso. The experiment needs to be brought into society, so put a scientist near it. Finally, give it a name. Put letters on the canvas, then flip the switch. The Starite appears, and the level is completed.

Metaforest is the tenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a giant forest made entirely of trees, and contains the Grave Manor area.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M6

SHARD 1

In the beginning of the level, a camper needs help keeping bugs off. Hand him some bug repellent to obtain the “Bug-Proof” shard.

SHARD 2

That camper doesn’t seem very comfortable; get him a bed to nab the “Sleeping in the Elements” shard.

SHARD 3

The old man with the chef hat has lost his sense of smell. Get him a pig to root for truffles and grab the “Truffle Hunter” shard.

SHARD 4

See that hippie near the tree? That’s Maxwell’s brother Serenity. Help him keep the lumberjack from chopping down the tree by handing the lumberjack a sleeping pill. This gets you the “Save the Owls” shard, and also unlocks Serenity as an Avatar!

SHARD 5

Up on the ledge near Serenity is a reporter who thinks he’s found a sasquatch den, but has no way to record it. Hand him a camera to get the “Photographic Evidence” shard.

SHARD 6

Those two wolves want to raise a human cub. Give them a baby for the “Raised by Wolves” shard.

SHARD 7

The children on the ledge above need help with their treehouse. Fix it up by providing a dad for the “Fixer-Upper” shard.

SHARD 8

Next to the children is Maxwell’s short brother Patches. His kite has gotten stuck in the tree and he needs it back. Grab a jetpack and get it for the “Patches’ Kite” shard. This also unlocks him as an Avatar!

SHARD 9

There’s a tree below Patches; it needs pollinating. Put a bee near it for the “Flowers into Fruit” shard.

SHARD 10

Give the “awake” adjective to the lumberjack, or reset the level. That lumberjack is actually Maxwell’s brother Jack. He has to cut down the tree, but he’s forgotten his tools. Give him a saw for the “Just Doing My Job” shard. Congrats, Jack is now unlocked as an Avatar!

Starites

At the very beginning of the level, the “The Merit Badge” Starite quest appears. To begin, the scout needs to carve a log. Give him a saw, and he’ll proceed to fire safety, where you can use a fire extinguisher to put the mannequin out. Bandage the mannequin up and proceed to the shooting range, where you’ll hand the scout a helmet and a bow, which in turn makes the Starite appear.

Grave Manor is the eleventh level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s located inside the Metaforest area.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M7

SHARD 1

That gravedigger found a coffin that brings things to life! Place a zombie inside and after a second it’ll pop out, earning you the “Buried Alive” shard.

SHARD 2

Near the fireplace are Maxwell’s invisible twins, Mix and Mox. They need something to play with! Give them a game and they’ll disappear, giving you the “Come Play With Us” shard and unlocking Mix and Mox as Avatars.

SHARD 3

The priest at the end of the hallway wants deliverance, so hand him some holy water for the “Holier Than Thou” shard.

SHARD 4

In the room upstairs sits a piano. Play it and monsters appear, giving you the “Do the Mash” shard.

SHARD 5

In the room with the suit of armor, summon a collector and the armor moves, getting you the “Secret Passage” shard.

SHARD 6

Head back downstairs, then through the hallway to see Mix and Mox with a black widow that wants to be more frightening. Edit it to be terrifying to get the “Arachnophobia” shard.

SHARD 7

Downstairs, that dirty mirror needs cleaning. Wipe it with a washcloth or place a mirror in front of it and get the “Maxwell’s Evil Twin” shard.

SHARD 8

Out on the roof sits a gargoyle that wants to be revived. Hit it with some lightning or give it the “alive” adjective to get the “Reanimator” shard.

SHARD 9

This attic needs to be creepier. Put a ghost or skeleton up there for the “Bump In the Night” shard.

SHARD 10

Down in the basement, a shambler is trapped by a flame. Get TNT or dynamite to blast the wall down and get the “Horror in the Basement” shard.

Starite

The Starite can be found in the attic, where ghosts will invade the house. First, Maxwell needs to see the ghosts, so give him a fortune teller or magic goggles. First, the girl ghost needs three things to play with. Three different toys will work, such as a ball, blocks and toy car. She’ll disappear, and a bride will appear. She needs three wedding items, so a husband, cake, and flowers will suffice. Next is a priest, who’s been trapped for ages. Give him three holy items, like holy water, a cross, and a candle. Finally, there’s a knight who claims to have been there the longest. Give him family members, like a son, wife, and daughter. The “Ghost Hunter” Starite will appear.

M8

SHARD 1

A medusa has turned these three tourists into stone! Give her a mate, such as a cthulhu, and you’ll get the “Heartbreak Medusa” shard. One of the frozen tourists was Maxwell’s brother Razer, who is now unlocked in Avatars.

SHARD 2

That fisher has been out all day, but there’s nothing to catch. Write in a fish or shark for the “Stocking the Lake” shard.

SHARD 3

That undead opera singer feels too hideous to be loved. Modify him to be handsome and get the “Phantom of the Grotto” shard.

SHARD 4

Up on the hill, red riding hood needs supplies for her grandmother. Give her a cookie for the “A Snack for Grandma” shard.

SHARD 5

The flower girl wants to make a wish in the well, so give her a penny for the “Make a Wish” shard.

SHARD 6

Red Riding Hood’s grandma feels her house is too plain. Modify it or use some paint on it for the “A Touch of Color” shard.

SHARD 7

On the rock ledge, the witch needs to stop the kids from eating her house. Modify it to be steel to get the “Homewreckers” shard.

Starite

On the hill sits a pioneer. He needs help on the Oregon trail. To get the “Moving West” Starite. First he’ll need a profession. Give him a hoe to make him a farmer. However, his ox will die of typhoid. Provide him with a creature such as a horse to pull the wagon. Next he’ll need someone who can teach him how to catch food, so give him a hunter friend. He’ll get his food, but he requests a home. Give him a cabin and the Starite appears.

Sir Guillemet’s Castle is the thirteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a giant castle with 4 floors.

This level is unlocked after 8 Starites are found.

M9

SHARD 1

Three orcs need help sneaking past a knight. Either subdue the knight or give the orcs disguises such as hats to get the “Orcs in Disguise” shard.

SHARD 2

Reset the level and help out the knight instead. He needs something to destroy the orcs, so give him a tank to get the “Orcs on the Bridge” shard.

SHARD 3

Next you’ll come across Maxwell’s brother Larp. He requests something to entertain the king, so give him a horn and you’ll get both the “Comedy is Hard” shard and Larp as an Avatar!

SHARD 4

Downstairs, an assassin (who looks suspiciously like Ezio) needs help breaking his friend out of jail. Summon a lockpick and obtain the “Dungeon Break” shard. Turns out, the assassin’s friend was Maxwell’s suspicious sibling Shadow. He’s been unlocked as an Avatar!

SHARD 5

Upstairs there’s a princess who hates her formal clothes. Give her a miniskirt for the “The Royal Wardrobe” shard.

SHARD 6

Up in the turret, an archer needs to mow down the invaders. Hand him a machine gun to get the “Manticores on the Roof” shard.

SHARD 7

On the top floor of the castle sits a group of people about to witness a beheading. However, the milkmaid is a friend of the criminal. Help her free him by subduing the executioner with a tranquilizer dart for the “Pardon Me” shard.

SHARD 8

Find the wizard in the tower. He needs an ancient ingredient, so give him a dinosaur bone to get the “The Philosopher’s Stone” shard.

STARITE

Go find the dragon egg on the second floor. For the “The Orphaned Dragon” Starite, you’ll need to turn a lizard into a dragon. First it’ll need something magical, so give it a potion. Next it needs something to give it holy breath, so a c’rosswill suffice. Finally, you must train the dragon by placing a vampire in the wooden cage. Once the vampire is defeated, you’ll get the Starite!

The Saurus Park is the fourteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. A park filled with prehistoric reptiles, The Saurus Park does indeed contain Jurassic Park references.

This level is unlocked after 12 Starites are found.

M10

SHARD 1

At the very beginning of the level you’ll encounter a crying boy who lost his purple toy pterodactyl. It’s on a platform above him. Create a jetpack, fly up and get it, and return it to him for the “Lost Toy” shard.

SHARD 2

A pterodactyl grabs the boy, a little above where the boy originally was… Make the pterodactyl drop him by modifying it to be kind, getting you the “Terrible Misconception” shard.

SHARD 3

There’s an archaeologist on the far right; he needs something that sucks blood to clone a dinosaur. Give him a bat (animal) for the “Dinosaur Respawn” shard.

SHARD 4

Above the tank sits a diver. She’s trying to teach the plesiosaurus how to play basketball, so give her a large ball for the “Teaching an Old Dino New Tricks” shard.

SHARD 5

Below the tank is a sheep. It’s not going to fit in at the exhibit as is, so modify it to be ancient and reptilian to obtain the “Fauxsaurus” shard.

SHARD 6

To the right of the sheep is Maxwell’s brother Rex. Stop him from hunting the dinosaurs by stealing his bow. You’ll get the “No Hunting Zone” shard, as well as the ability to play as Rex in Avatars.

SHARD 7

To the right of Rex is a tyrannosaurus with meat stuck in its throat. Sprinkle some pepper about to get the “Remember to Chew” shard.

SHARD 8

In the top left of the level, a scientist warns you of a disaster a butterfly will cause in the future. Kill the butterfly with a sword to get the “The Butterfly Effect” shard.

SHARD 9

Near the beginning of the level is an underground container. Reset the level and fill it with a cow to get the “Clever Girl” shard.

SHARD 10

Lastly, a teacher under the plesio tank wants to play with a dinosaur that won’t hurt them. Write in a friendly stegosaurus and snag the “Dinosaur Field Trip” shard.

STARITE 1

An allosaurus has been loosed in the park! To start, you’ll need someone who can track it down. Write a hunter, but he’ll need a tool. Give him a magnifying glass and he’ll find it hiding behind a bush. However, he’ll need a lure. Place meat in the cage and the allosaurus will head that way. Unfortunately, he’ll ignore the bait and go after the hunter. Give the zookeeper some meat to get its attention. The dinosaur will get tired, so shoot it with a tranquilizer dart to complete the challenge and get the “Allosaurus On the Loose” Starite.

STARITE 2

In a homage to The Five Million Dollar Man, the “The Five Million Dollar Dino” Starite petitions you to rebuild a T-Rex. Start with a tail, then add an engine and a saw. Finally he’ll need some fuel; place gasoline inside him to get the Starite.

Anaphora Falls is the fifteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. A waterfall paradise, Anaphora Falls contains The Saurus Park.

This level is unlocked after 12 Starites are found.

M11

SHARD 1

The sunbather at the starting point needs something to help him tan; handing him a mirror will get you the “Vitamin D Please” shard.

SHARD 2

Below sits a frog that needs help crossing the river. Put a bridge there for “Classic Retro Game Hopping” shard.

SHARD 3

Those piranhas in the water are distinctly vegetarian, so throw some lettuce in the water for the “Feeding Picky Piranhas” shard.

SHARD 4

Tragically, the bridesmaid on the ground has died. Revive her by modifying her to be living for the “Honeymoon Heartbreak” shard. Alternatively, if you’re feeling more sadistic you can kill her boyfriend for the same effect.

SHARD 5

That boat is stuck on a rock, but longs to get back in the water. Destroy the rock to get the “River Speed Bump” shard.

SHARD 6

Head back to near where you began. Add the “burning” adjective to the torch. Then Empty the treasure chest and a booby (the bird) will come out, resulting in your getting the “Booby Trapped” shard.

SHARD 7

Near the top of the level is a tourist who needs help taking a photo with a native. Hand him a camera for the “Photo Shy Shaman” shard.

SHARD 8

Next to the tourist is a shaman who wants to show you the dance of his people. Put a boombox near him for the “Rain Dance” shard.

SHARD 9

The pair of gorillas wants to include you in their club; sadly they only allow gorillas. Put on a gorilla suit to get the “Joining the Troop” shard.

SHARD 10

Up on the ledge above the gorillas sits an elephant who wants to paint. Give him a paintbrush and claim the “Upcoming Artist L.E. Phant” shard.

STARITE 1

To obtain the “Teaching a Businessman to Fish” Starite, you need to help a businessman become fish-savvy. To begin, dress him in hat. Next you’ll need to give him a fishing pole, followed by placing a worm in the water. He’ll catch the food, but a rainbow trout will appear. Place a rainbow bait in the water and he’ll proceed to nab that as well. Finally, a fudge megalodon will appear. Toss in a chocolate and the Starite appears.

STARITE 2

For the “Primate Pals” Starite you’ll need to create the human equivalent of each monkey. First, put a rocker on the stage, followed by a witch, then a nerd. Finally, write Maxwell to make the Starite appear.

Ruins of Ellipsis is the sixteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It is a relatively small level, consisting of an overground, a temple and an underground passage.

This level is unlocked after 12 Starites are found.

M12

SHARD 1

Start by talking to the gardener by the plant. The carnivorous plant could cure diseases, but she can’t get it to bloom. Put some steak near it to get the “Green Thumb” shard.

For the 3DS version, there seems to be a bug and steak does not work. Simply write bug and it will work.

SHARD 2

Those ants’ leader has died; create a new king by giving one of them a crown for the “The Ant’s Throne” shard.

SHARD 3

Underneath the tree sits a pigeon. He’s jealous of the exotic birds, so modify him to be iridescent to grab the “Birds Not of a Feather” shard.

SHARD 4

Head to the right where you’ll find a wannabe Jane Goodall. She needs to study the apes, so give her a notebook for the “Primate Research” shard.

SHARD 5

Head inside the tree where you’ll find a necromancer who wants to revive his king. Look around the level for the three pieces: one in the passage to the right, one above ground near the ape researcher, and one on top of the temple. Bringing them all to him nets you the “Crystal King” shard.

SHARD 6

In a passage past the necromancer is an archaeologist who keeps getting shot with darts. Take out the machine that the hula dancer to the right is operating to get the “Dodging Darts” shard.

SHARD 7

Remember that plant you fed for the first shard? It’s chasing the botanist around, trying to eat her. Satisfy the plant with ham to get the “Red Thumb” shard.

Starite

Create your own band of heroes by equipping them all. First, give the warrior an axe. Hand the rogue a bow, followed by a tome for the mage. Finally, give the healer some medicine and they’re off to fight the dragon. Once it’s defeated, you’ll nab the “Scribblequest” Starite.

Payper N. Penitentiary is the seventeenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a huge prison with a watchtower. The name is a play on “paper and pen” (Payper N. PENitentiary! GEDDIT?!).

This level is unlocked after 16 Starites are found.

M13

SHARD 1

First, start with the sniper in the watchtower. He, uh, needs to use the facilities, so place a toilet up there with him and grab the “Don’t Forget to Wash Your Hands” shard.

SHARD 2

SHARD 6

SHARD 6

SHARD 7

Inside the cell to the left is Maxwell’s brother Bruiser. Sneak a weapon inside the cake, such as a gun, and once it’s delivered to him, you’ll get both the “Tasty Escape” shard and unlock Bruiser as an Avatar.

SHARD 8

The garden gnome wants something to remind her of home; show her a bush and grab the “Far From Gnome” shard.

The easter bunny is depressed due to the plain egg. Cheer him up by handing him rainbow paint and collect the “Splash of Color” shard.

SHARD 10

The tooth fairy needs one of the werewolf’s teeth, but he’s too ferocious while he’s conscious. Shoot him with a tranquilizer dart and get the “Werewolf Fang” shard.

STARITE 1

Click the football player on the second floor to start the “Cellmates” quest. Each criminal needs a cellmate that shares a common interest. To begin, give the football player a coach. The gate will open and you can proceed to the next room where a halfling needs a companion. Give her a wizard and go through the doorway. The saxophonist needs someone in his band; write in a guitarist and continue. The actress needs someone to practice lines with, write in a director and keep going to talk with the scientist. Give him a scientific partner such as a botanist and go to the next room, where you’ll meet a cosplayer. Write in a gamer, and in addition to making the Starite appear.

STARITE 2

To get the “Prison Break” shard, Maxwell must escape the penitentiary. Give the guard a stool and he’ll sit down and unlock the gate. A ghost will appear downstairs, but he can’t open the door without a physical body. Spawn a corpse and proceed. The next criminal is incredibly hungry, so give him a steak and he’ll open the gate. Downstairs, a con man will help Maxwell, but only if he can give the con man a disguise. Hand him a police suit and keep on trucking. Finally, the programmer misses his pet, so give him a dog and leave the prison, collecting the Starite on the way.

STARITE 3

The last Starite of the level lies at the end of The Gauntlet. Place something such as an anvil on the button to open the door, and put the fires out with a hose. Get through the room with the spiky platforms by tripping the first wire and stepping back; then trip the second one and run to the space between the traffic cones. Once it’s safe, head downstairs to the room. Wait for the robot to finish firing, then run quickly through the room before all the lights turn on. The game wants you to grab the Starite, so trip the wire and watch as it’s destroyed. However, you can still make things right. Grab the key and head upstairs. Use a magnet to pull the spiky steel ball to where the Starite was, then go back down and to the left. Pull the lever. The gate rises, but is electrified. Modify Maxwell to be tiny and run underneath. Use a jetpack to fly up and press the red button. Wait for the TNT to explode, then proceed to cross the roof in time to the lightning. Time it right and fly down to the time machine. Move the steel ball away from where it was, then use the time machine. Open the door with the lever, and grab the Starite. Phew!

Payper Plains is the eighteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It’s a fairly small, flat area filled with carnival people.

Bullet Point Bayou is the nineteenth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. A fairly small and straightforward level, the entire lower half of the area is filled with murky green sludge water.

This level is unlocked after 16 Starites are found.

SHARD 1

Right at the beginning of the level is Maxwell’s brother Scratch. He really needs to, erm, use the facilities, but the outhouse is too disgusting. Give Scratch a janitor and he’ll rush to the newly-cleansed bathroom. You get both the “Not Very Sanitary” shard and Scratch as an Avatar.

SHARD 2

Down in the water is a turtle who’s mistaken himself for a duckling. Help him fit in by giving him a beak to get the “Ugly Duckling” shard.

SHARD 3

Woodpeckers have made a nest out of that living treant. Get them away by putting a redwood nearby and collect the “Aviary Annoyance” shard.

SHARD 5

SHARD 6

An alligator is floating in the far bottom left of the level. He’s starving, so toss in some meat and get the “Feed the 'Gator” shard.

Starite

To get the “They Walk Among Us” Starite, Maxwell will have to become a makeshift Man in Black. Aliens are disguised as earthly beings, so to root them out, Maxwell must use his powers of logic. The first alien is afraid of bright lights. That’s obviously the vampire on the overturned boat, so place a sun next to him and he’ll shed his disguise. The next alien is afraid of loud noises, so grab a drumset and play it near the baby. The following alien is terrified of water, so place a storm over the cat. Finally, the last alien is afraid of heights. However, there’s an imposter in our midst! The secret agent is actually an alien! The Listy Colon is the twentieth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. The entire level consists of a pirate ship, of which Maxwell’s brother Yarrr seems to be the captain.

This level is unlocked after 16 Starites are found.

M16

SHARD 1

On top of the ship are a ninja and a pirate. They need a tool to fight with, so give the ninja a katana or the pirate a cutlass for the “Picking Sides” shard.

SHARD 2

A rogue is on top of the mast to the upper right. He needs you to retrieve the treasure map he’s lost. It’s locked in the chest in the bottom right of the level. First, give Maxwell some wings, then fly down towards the water. Kill the shark with a harpoon gun and unlock the chest. Grab the map and bring it to the rogue for the “Treasure Map” shard.

SHARD 3

There’s an assassin locked inside the prison. Place a crate inside and he’ll make his escape, netting you the “Stealthy Escape” shard.

SHARD 4

The pirate to the right of the main mast needs something to craft a leg out of; get him a log for the “Peg Leg” shard.

SHARD 5

The green conquistador near the pirate needs something that will prevent him from catching scurvy. Serve him a glass of orange juice to obtain the “Scurvy Dogs” shard.

SHARD 6

Flying above is a seagull with dreams of being a parrot. Modify him to be colorful to get the “Replacement Parrot” shard.

SHARD 7

In the bottom level to the far left is a hacker and a laptop. Prevent internet piracy by smashing the laptop with a sledgehammer to get the “A Different Kind of Piracy” shard.

SHARD 8

To the right of the would-be hacker is a deaf sailor. Apparently he’s heard mistakenly that he needs to swab the duck (yes, duck); oblige him anyway by handing him a mop to collect the “Swab the Duck?” shard.

SHARD 9

The two warriors at the table to the right need some pieces to play a game. Hand them some dice and get the “Sore Loser” shard.

SHARD 10

Standing near the cannon is Maxwell’s brother Yarrr. The ship is out of ammunition, so toss a bomb in the cannon to obtain the “Need More Ammo” shard and unlock Yarrr as an Avatar.

SHARD 11

The crow in the, er, crow’s nest seems to have mistaken it for an actual nest. Make him content anyway by placing a stick inside and grab the “Crow’s Nest” shard.

STARITE 1

Interact with the ghost in the middle of the ship to start the “The Cursed Crew” quest. Each member of the crew has become cursed, and the only way to revert them is to give them something they used to enjoy. To begin, the skeleton warrior willl need a battleaxe. The next member loved his facial hair, but it’s completely gone now. Put a beard on the zombie to lift the next curse. The following crew member has been robbed of his body and turned into a wisp. Place a mirror near him to break the next curse. The smartest member has been turned into a mindless ghoul. Give him a brain to proceed to the final challenge, where the ghost captain must be reunited with his parrot. Write one in, but in a twist of events, the captain reveals that they’re all really ghosts! The captain disintegrates and the Starite appears.

STARITE 2

Good sailors are hard to find. Help make it easier for the captain and create some. To complete the “Where Can I Find Some Sailors?” Starite, begin by helping the slacker kid who inherited the ship find someone to steer. Place a sailor at the wheel. Next we’ll need someone to keep track of the booty, so put a treasurer in the treasure room. Weapons are an important part of a ship, so place a warrior in the bottom left of the ship. Keep the crew well-fed by putting a chef in the kitchen. Next we’ll need a captive to keep in the brig, so place a native inside. Finally, place a navigator in the crow’s nest and the Starite will appear.

After the cutscene that introduces the plot of the game ends, you will find yourself on a farm. Your first objective is to walk over toward Edwin, Maxwell’s brother, near the right side of the level. On-screen images will instruct you on how to walk. Speak to Edwin, and you’ll be prompted to assist him with his award-winning pig. You must do this by finishing a number of tasks.

“Turn the runt of the litter into an award winning pig! Write an object to help Maxwell scrub the pig!”
“Type something to clean the pig!”
This can be anything from a “brush” to a “toothbrush” (don’t limit your creativity). Alternate Items: Soap, Shampoo

Remove the object you used to clean the pig by dropping it in the trash can.

“The pig looks great! Now use an adjective to increase the pig’s size!”
Do this by adding an adjective to the pig that will increase its size. Type anything from “big” to “ginormous”.

“Help the pig win the talent competition! Use an adjective and a noun to create wings large enough to carry the pig!”
“Hints are unlocked over time! Click on the associated object to view its hint!”
Click the pig to view the new hint!

“I need wings large enough to help me fly.”
Add wings large enough to help the pig fly by creating an object such as “big wings”. Any adjective that implies that the wings will be big is good enough.

“The magic backpack stores objects for later! Throw the wings in the backpack and wait for the judges to arrive!”
Place the wings you’ve created into your magic backpack. The judges will arrive just seconds after you’ve done this. Now you must take the wings from the magic backpack and attach them to the pig. A short cutscene will begin in which the judges talk for a while, and then the pigs are placed in order based on size. Your pig will win the contest, and a Starite will appear in front of your pig. Walk over to the Starite and pick it up.

“Earn Shards by helping people! Ten shards will form a Starite! Use Starite Vision to find shards!”
Activate Starite Vision by clicking the Starite icon, which is next to the backpack icon in the top left corner of the screen. Then click on Edwin, who is highlighted in gold. A hint will appear:

“Help the sprout grow!”
Turn off Starite Vision, open your notepad, and create an object that will help Edwin’s sprout grow. This can be anything from “water” to “rain”.

Alternate Items: Water Bottle, River

The sprout will turn into a vicious monster plant and scare away Edwin and the Sad Girl.

“The plant scared away the other shard opportunity! Reset the level to return all objects to their original state, while keeping your earned shards!”
Go to the main menu and restart the level. Don’t worry-- this will save your current progress.

“While the notepad can create many things, vulgarity, copyrighted materials, and proper nouns are not allowed.”
Using Starite Vision on the Sad Girl will bring up the hint:

“My kitty is stuck on top of the tree! Get her for me!”
Do this by creating anything from a “ladder” to a flight of “big stairs”. Anything that will allow you to reach the top of the tree works.

Alternate Method: Just climb up the tree without conjuring up anything. Then retrieve the cat and climb back down.

Grab the cat, and bring it safely back to the Sad Girl.

“Learning the basics was just the first step towards saving Lily! Use the bus stop to begin your journey in Capital City!”
Completing this level will allow you to play as Maxwell’s brother Edwin. Simply click Maxwell and select “Pick Avatar” to play as any unlocked brother.

In order to reach the next level, walk all the way to the right side of Edwin’s farm until you reach the bus stop. Use the bus stop to travel to Capital City.

Capital City is the second level in Scribblenauts Unlimited.

A cockroach can be found on this level near Shard 2. It’s not recommended that you create another cockroach. Creating another cockroach will allow the two to multiply extremely quickly. If this happens, no Shards will be able to be earned unless the level is reset.

After finding all of the Shards and the Starite in Capital City, the game will bring you back to Edwin’s Farm to speak to Lily, who will introduce more levels for you to play on, along with a map to provide quicker travel between areas.

M17

SHARD 1

Upon arriving in Capital City, you’ll find a Bored Boy just to the left of the bus stop. The hint you’ll find says:

“I want someone to entertain me!”

This one is simple. Create anything the boy would find mildly entertaining such as an “elephant” or “balloon”. Playing a guitar in front of him, however, does not work. Upon success, you will earn the first Shard found in Capital City.

Alternate Items: Joker, Clown

SHARD 2

Further to the left of the now Happy Boy is an illegally-parked car. The goal here is to move the car away from the area that’s illegal to park in. Simply create a “towtruck”, attach the chain to the illegally-parked car, and remove the car from the area. A security guard will appear out of nowhere, get upset that the car has been removed, and you’ll be awarded with the second Shard found in Capital City.

Alternate Method: Make a rope and attach it to the car and then to you. Then, simply walk away from the car to pull it with you.

The security guard will appear as an enemy, and throw a binder at you whenever you get near him. This will deal damage to you, so be careful. Adding the adjective “happy” to the security guard will render him harmless.

SHARD 3

Further to the left of Shard 2 is graffiti located on the wall of a building. Scrub the graffiti off with something like a “washcloth” or “soap” and you will be awarded with the third Shard found in Capital City.

Note: Water items, such as rain and water bottle, will not work.

Erasing the graffiti will cause a park bench, light pole, and two trees to appear out of nowhere.

SHARD 4

Near the graffiti is Maxwell’s brother Clark flying in circles. To the left of him is a telephone booth. Create and place any human being into the telephone booth such as a “boy” or “sidekick” in order to turn them into his sidekick. Doing so will earn you the fourth Shard found in Capital City.

Note: Any type of human will work, including veteranarian, trashman, and engeneer.

After creating an accomplice for Clark, a cat burglar will appear and Clark and his accomplice will chase after him. Doing this will unlock Clark as an Avatar.

SHARD 5

Near the telephone booth is a switch and a pool of water. Place any object into the fountain, pull the switch, and a fountain for the city will be created. Literally anything can be placed into the pool of water and turned into a fountain. This will earn your the fifth Shard found in Capital City. It will look cooler if the item is made out of stone, so try a stone elephant.

After creating the fountain, a schoolboy and schoolgirl will appear and throw money into the fountain.

SHARD 6

Beyond Shard 5’s fountain and the Starite’s carnival, you’ll find a dock. At the end of the dock is an old man who wants to sail across the sea. Create any source of water transportation such as a “sailboat” and place it in the water. The man will hop into it and sail off, and you’ll be awarded with the sixth Shard found in Capital City.

Alternate Items: Boat, Raft, Cruise

SHARD 7

Backtrack a bit until you reach the telephone booth at Shard 5. To the right of the telephone booth is an entrance into a building. Enter it, and you’ll find yourself at the roof of that building with two unhappy tourists who can’t see the view because the clouds are in the way. Create something that can blow the clouds out of the way such as a “fan” and you’ll be awarded with the seventh Shard found in Capital City.

After the clouds are blown out of the way, a rainbow and sun will appear, and the tourists will become happy.

Alternate Items: Wind’, Breeze

SHARD 8

A gorilla can be found on top of the hospital near the beginning of this level. Pan the camera up toward it, and you’ll find that the gorilla wants to be “kinged”. Place a “princess” next to him or give him a “crown”, and you’ll be awarded with the eighth and final Shard found in Capital City.

After this, an airplane will appear and begin shooting the ape. The ape will then die, and the princess will escape.

Alternate Items: Tiara, Queen

Starite

Speak to the man found beyond the fountain at Shard 5. This will begin the carnival games. The first game requires you to throw something to knock over the bottles without crossing the barrier. Simply create something that can be thrown such as a “ball”. Click the bottom, second-to-right bottle in order to be given the “throw” command. Throw the ball at the bottles and knock them over. A giant ball isn’t necessary, as the bottles will all break once one is broken.

Note: Any throwable item will work. A ball isn’t necessary.

The next game requires you to throw something to pop the balloons being carried by the clown without crossing the barrier. Create something sharp that can be thrown such as a “dart”.

Note: Once again, there isn’t any limit. Anything that can be thrown at a balloon will work.

The next game requires you to throw something to hit the dunk tank without crossing the barrier. If the ball you created earlier is still around, just throw that at the dunk tank.

Note: Anything throwable will work

You will be awarded a Starite for completing this task.

Sub Level

There is a sub-level that can be accessed on the world map. This level is called Capital City Runoff.

Capital City Runoff is a relatively small level, as it’s actually a sub-level. It’s also a pretty unimportant level, as there are no Starites and only 6 Shards to collect. Nevertheless, they are required for a 100% playthrough. The level can be accessed through the world map.

M18

SHARD 1

The pufferfish near the start point is being made fun of by the other fish. Modify him to be giant and scary to get the “Scaredy Fish” shard.

SHARD 2

The anemone hanging off the pipe is ready for someone new to move in. Conjure up a clownfish to get the “Vacant Anemone” shard.

SHARD 3

The cage at the bottom of the sea floor is a lobster trap. Save the lobsters by filling the cage with a stone and you’ll obtain the “I Can’t Believe It’s Not Lobster” shard.

SHARD 4

At the very bottom of the level is Maxwell’s brother Tempo. He asks you to “be a bro” and free him. Cut the rope he’s attached to and you’ll receive the “Crime Doesn’t Pay” shard, as well as unlock Tempo in Avatars!

St. Asterisk is the third level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It is a large hospital, where Maxwell’s brother Kenpo seems to be a patient.

M19

SHARD 1

The first Shard is located near the entrance of the hospital. Located just to the right of the front desk is an injured man on a surgical table and a surgeon. The surgeon needs something that she can use to perform surgery. Give her a sharp item such as a “scalpel”. If you give her an innapropriate sharp item such as a “saw” and the man on the surgical table will sprint out of the hospital with the surgeon chasing behind him. Either way, you will earn you the first Shard found in St. Asterisk. Turns out that the patient was Maxwell’s brother Kenpo. He’s unlocked as an Avatar!

Alternate Items: Knife, Scissors, Laser

SHARD 2

The second Shard is located on the second floor of the hospital. On the second floor is a clown lying on a hospital bed. You can make the clown laugh by creating a “comedian” or by wearing a “clown nose”. This will earn you the second Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Alternate Items: Most items with the adjective funny, Joker

SHARD 3

The third Shard is located on the third floor of the hospital. On the third floor is a nurse being chased by a heart. Kill the heart or add the adjective “dead” to the heart and you will earn the third Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Alternate Method: Attacking and killing the heart also works. Create a gun or knife and kill the heart with the item.

SHARD 4

The fourth Shard is located just to the right of the third Shard on the third floor. Here you will find an orthopaedist in need of new equipment for the hospital. Give him any tool such as “scissors” and you will earn the fourth Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Alternate Items: Scalpel, Knife

SHARD 5

The fifth Shard is located on the fourth floor of the hospital. On the fourth floor are two patients who would be motivated to win a race if they saw something painted with their team colors. One patient’s team color is blue, the other patient’s team color is red. Paint something red or blue, or create anything with one of those colors such as a “blue flag”, and you will earn the fifth Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Alternate Method: You can also change the adjectives of current things lying around. There is no need to make a completely new item.

SHARD 6

The sixth Shard is located on the roof of the hospital. On the fifth floor is a paramedic who’s left what she was delivering to the transplant patient behind. Create something that can be transplanted such as a “heart” and you will earn the sixth Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Alternate Items: Lung, Kidney, Intestine

SHARD 7

The seventh Shard is located just to the right of the Sixth shard on the roof of the hospital. Interact with the stork here, and the stork will tell you that it has lost what it was supposed to be delivering. Give it something to deliver like a “baby” and you will earn the seventh and final Shard found in St. Asterisk.

Starite

The Starite in this level is found near the entrance of the hospital. Head right until you reach a man next to an ambulance. Talk to him, and he’ll tell you to help the patients that are coming into the hospital. After accepting this goal, you’ll be dressed as a doctor and must help those being brought into the hospital.

The first patient is a sick kitten. Remedy the kitten’s sickness with “medicine”, and the next patient will be brought in.

The second patient is a tooth ache. Remedy the tooth ache with a “dentist” or give the nearby doctor “pliers”, and the next patient will be brought in.

The third patient is a rusty robot. Rid the robot’s rust with a “scientist” or “rust remover”, and the next patient will be brought in.

The fourth patient is a book missing its last chapter. Create a “writer” to write the final chapter, or give the nearby man a “pen”, and the next patient will be brought in.

The fifth patient is a car with something broken underneath its hood. Remedy the broken car with a “mechanic”, and you will be given the Starite.

The Virgule Gallery is the fourth level in Scribblenauts Unlimited. It is a large art gallery filled with many varied types of people.

M20

SHARD 1

The first shard can be found at the entrance to the gallery. There is a curator near the entrance who needs help figuring out which of the artifacts in front of here is a fraud. Create an archaeologist and he will discover which is the fraud, revealing the “Counterfeit Artifact” shard.

SHARD 2

The second shard can be found just to the right of the first shard. Here you will find an archaeologist who needs something to connect the bones on the floor in front of him together. Create “glue” and place it on the bones to earn the “Dem Bones” shard.

SHARD 3

The third shard can be found just to the right of the second shard. Here you will find a penguin who needs help getting past the guard and the security camera so it can take back its diamond. Add the adjective invisible to the penguin to get the “Arctic Heist” shard.

SHARD 4

The fourth shard can be found just to the right of the guard and security camera. Here you will find Artie, who thinks the girl in the painting behind him looks lonely. Create a boy and place it in the painting to earn the “Still-life Solitude” shard, as well as Artie’s Avatar.

SHARD 5

The fifth shard can be found on the second floor of the gallery. Wax Columbus can be found near the top of the flight of stairs here, and he needs you to finish his exhibit. Create a boat and place it on him to earn the “Brave New World” shard.

SHARD 6

The sixth shard can be found in the exhibit just to the left of the Wax Columbus exhibit. Here you will find Wax Cleopatra who needs you to finish her exhibit. Create a pyramid and place it next to her to earn the “Queen of the Nile” shard.

SHARD 7

The seventh shard can be found in the exhibit just to the left of the Wax Cleopatra exhibit. Here you will find Wax Benjamin Franklin who needs you to finish his exhibit. Create a toy kite and place it next to him to earn the “The Key to Success” shard.

SHARD 8

The final shard can be found in the exhibit just to the left of the Wax Benjamin Franklin exhibit. Here you will find Wax George Washington who needs you to finish his exhibit. Create an axe and place it next to him to earn the “Never Tell a Lie” shard.

Starites

The Starite in this level is found near the beginning. Just beyond the first shard you should find an image in the background with a blue star indicator hovering over it. Activating it will begin a task called “Finnicky Patrons”. In this task you must help Maxwell provide art that will be sure to please patrons of the museum. There are multiple possible combinations of visitors, so follow the appropriate instructions for whoever shows up.

Robot- Create a gear and place it in the frame.

Mummy- Create a pyramid and place it in the frame.

Headless Horseman- Create a head and place it in the frame.

Mrs. Claus- Create Santa Claus and place him in the frame.

Gnome- Create a flower and place it in the frame.

Invisible Man- Create nearly anything with the adjective invisible and place it in the frame.

Treant- Create a tree and place it in the frame.

Troglodyte- Create a bone and place it in the frame.

Ghost- Create a spirit and place it in the frame.

Zombie- Create a girl and place her in the frame.

After pleasing every patron, the Starite will appear to the far left near the window.
